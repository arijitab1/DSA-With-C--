Operator                                 Precedence

!, +, -(unary operator)           first Right to Left
*, /, %                           Second    L to R
+, -                              third     L to R
<, <=, >=, >                      fourth    L to R
==, !=                            fifth     L to R
&&                                sixth     L to R 
||                                seventh   L to R 
= (assignment Operator)           last      R to L
